Welcome! My name is Yuwei Wan (万雨薇) and I am a Phd student in Computational Linguistics at City University of Hong Kong. Before this, I completed my Computer Science Master degree at Johns Hopkins and my Bachelor in Linguistics and Language Applications, as well as a minor in Computing at CityU.

My research interests lie in computational linguistics, or so called NLP in computer science, with rich experience in tasks like text classification, named entity recognition, machine translation and knowledge representation/acquisition. I'd love to explore interdisciplinary NLP task, like NLP in literature, social media, laws, biomedicine and material science. I also interested in applying linguistics knowledge in NLP models. Feel free to look at my research to learn more!
